Lucid is a minimalist timer app for macOS that floats above your workspace, giving you a clear sense of time without adding visual noise. Each timer lives in its own translucent window that you can move wherever it helps you most, staying visible while you work, create or rest.

Designed for macOS, Lucid feels at home alongside your other apps and adapts to different workflows with ease.

Made for people who work with time.

Lucid is for anyone who juggles tasks, breaks and routines throughout the day. Designers, developers, educators and students use it to keep track of focus sessions, meetings, workouts or even coffee brewing, without hiding the apps they care about.

Design Process

Lucid was designed around one idea: a timer should stay visible and helpful without competing with your work. Traditional timer apps often hide in the menu bar or live in a single fixed window. Lucid instead gives each timer its own lightweight, floating presence.

The interface focuses on clarity and motion. Translucent windows blend with the desktop, while typography and spacing keep the time readout easy to parse at a glance. Natural language input was introduced to reduce friction when setting timers, turning a common interaction into a simple sentence.
