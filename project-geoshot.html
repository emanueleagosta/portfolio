Geoshot - Map Snapshot

Capture beautiful map snapshots.

App Store Link ‚Üó

Geoshot is an iOS app for creating clean, shareable map images that tell the story of where you have been. It blends precise location selection, an interactive map, and simple customization so you can turn any place into a visual highlight.

Developed for travelers, hikers, and explorers, Geoshot makes location sharing feel intentional and crafted, not just another screenshot.

Made for people who love places.

Geoshot is for anyone who wants to remember and share where moments happened: a hike you finished, a trip you loved, or a spot you keep coming back to. Whether you are documenting your travels, creating posts for social media, or saving locations for yourself, Geoshot keeps the experience focused and visual.

Features

Create clean map snapshots.

Drop a pin on the map, adjust the view, and capture a snapshot without extra UI elements getting in the way. The result is a clean image that keeps the focus on the place you care about.

Mark locations with your own style.

Choose from customizable pins and colors using a built-in color picker. Each snapshot can reflect your aesthetic, from subtle tones to bold, high-contrast markers.

Capture precise compositions.

Turn on the grid overlay to preview exactly what will appear in your image before you capture it. The grid helps align the map for social posts, stories, or any layout where framing matters.

Built for iOS.

Pan, zoom, and explore using interactions that feel native on iOS. The map is responsive and smooth, making it easy to find and frame the location you have in mind.

Design Process

Geoshot was designed around a single idea: location sharing should feel deliberate and visually clear. The app evolved through iterations that simplified the interface and kept the map itself as the main character.

The interactive map is built on MapKit, with a focus on quick navigation, precise pin placement, and minimal chrome. Customization options such as pin colors were refined to give users control.

The grid overlay feature was introduced to solve a practical problem: social posts often crop or constrain images. By showing a grid before capture, Geoshot lets users frame their maps exactly as they will appear when shared.
